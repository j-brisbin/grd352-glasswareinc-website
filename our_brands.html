No Matter Your Production Needs, Glassware Inc. Has Them Covered

Our comprehensive, world-class brands can help you cover your production needs. Whether your next production is a large feature film or a small YouTube video, Glassware Inc. gives you the push to make your next production truly spectacular.

Glassware Productions

Glassware Productions is the main division of Glassware Inc. We distribute our productions through traditional and digital means. In addition to our own productions created by internal divisions, Glassware Productions funds smaller productions made by independent creators, giving them a platform to voice their ideas on a large scale.

Hourglass Pictures

Hourglass Pictures is the feature film division of Glassware Inc. It mainly specializes in funding independent films for theatrical, retail, and digital distribution. With a large distribution network, Glassware Inc. takes the brilliance that made our production division what it is today and applies it to feature films.
